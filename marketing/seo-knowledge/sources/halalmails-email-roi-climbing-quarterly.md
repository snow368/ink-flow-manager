## 核心内容

### Email 是唯一 ROI 应该持续上升的渠道

> Email is the only marketing channel where your ROI should be climbing every quarter
>
> - Meta gets more expensive
> - Google gets more expensive
> - Every other channel decays
> - Email is supposed to get cheaper as you invest in it
>
> If yours has been flat for 6 months, you're not running an [email program]

### 核心洞察
1. **Email 的边际成本递减** — 投得越多，ROI 越高
2. **其他渠道的边际成本递增** — Meta/Google 竞价越来越贵
3. **6 个月没增长 = 你的 email program 有问题** — 不是渠道问题
