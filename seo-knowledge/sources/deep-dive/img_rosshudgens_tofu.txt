what is crm
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. It refers to the technology,
strategies, and practices that businesses use to track and manage all interactions with
current and potential customers throughout their entire lifecycle. When people use the term
today, they are usually referring to a CRM system, which is a specialized software tool
designed to act as a centralized database for customer information.
